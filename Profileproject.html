Introduction

I offer highly individualized, private instruction to students of all ages and abilities. Whether I'm teaching chair yoga to a 98-year-old woman, a beginner with chronic pain, or a seasoned yogi looking to grow their practice through one-on-one instruction, I offer a therapeutic approach to healing & strengthening the body through mindful movement. By weaving together breath and body awareness, meditation, and deep rest, I aim to create a safe, nourishing space for students to practice self-love & self-care. I love working one-on-one with clients to help them feel better in their bodies. I offer public as well as private (in-person/skype/video) yoga classes, and guided meditation. My mission is to provide you with tools that will bring well-being to your mind, body, and spirit. I do this through yoga, meditation, and aromatherapy. Namaste

Benefits

Benefits of Private Yoga

Your private yoga teacher will be able to facilitate individualized teachings tailored to you specifically. They give you the ability to direct your own practice or to have a personal yoga trainer create a program that meets your needs.

Health Benefits from doing Yoga

  • Increases Energy Level
  • Relieves Stress, Anxiety, Tension, and Depression
  • Builds Strength, Flexibility, and Endurance
  • Speeds up Healing Process Due to Sports Injury
  • Lubricates Joints and Bones
  • Relieves Many Chronic Conditions (back pain, knee pain, and arthritis)

Yoga trains the mind in 5 key ways:

Self control - Determination - Concentration - Self Confidence - Patience
